Film Faced Shuttering Plywood in Madhya Pradesh

Multi Strong Plywood is the leading (IS: 4990 certified) Film Faced Shuttering Plywood Manufacturer in Madhya Pradesh. The offered film faced shuttering plywood is high-density wood adhered with BWP Grade Phenol formaldehyde resin and smoothed in a high densified heated press.

This film faced shuttering plywood has additional stability than usual film face ply due to immense pressure created for structurally durable and better bonding. While manufacturing, it is tested by ACC by pressure impregnation.

Special Features of Film Faced Shuttering Plywood:
  • • Its USP is 100% boiling waterproof, robust GSM phenolic film and excellent bolt holding capacity.
  • • The edges are resealed with acrylic paint to maintain moisture absorption and abrasion.
  • • The shuttering plywood is the most suitable for reusable.
  • • It has a lustrous glass surface that makes the construction process simple, smooth and affordable.

The provided film faced shuttering plywood can customize in various dimensions as per the necessities of our customers. Our valuable customers can also obtain Shuttering Plywood from us at competitive prices.
